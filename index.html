About US

We are Undergraduates of University of Colombo School of Computing. This application is created under professional practice course as a charity project.

This application is for both vendors and farmers. This applicatoin supports farmers to indicate themselves easily identify for the vendors. This application supports sinhala fonts and it is very easy to read for local users.

This application named as "smart finder" and there are three developers of this application. Sandun , Menuka and Thamail are the team members of this application. Our main aim is to support both vendors and farmers to easily do their task.
